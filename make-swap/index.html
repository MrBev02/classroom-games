Build a puzzle, swap it with a classmate

Type in some words (and a clue for each). Make it into a word search or a crossword, then share the link so someone else can solve it. Try theirs back.

  1. 1Add your words — a term plus a clue (the clue is what the solver reads).
  2. 2Press “Create share link” — you’ll get a link and a short code.
  3. 3Share it — paste the link into your class chat / Google Classroom, or hand over your laptop.
  4. 4Solve a classmate’s — open their link, switch between word search and crossword, and finish it.
